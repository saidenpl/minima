Okay, so you’ve got your Spotify premium account and are in a situation where a device able to play music better than your mobile, tablet or laptop is present. Sadly, the device neither has a Spotify client, nor uPnP media player or AirPlay capabilities…

While wondering how to play music from my laptop on a Linux MPD server that serves as a jukebox I came to one simple conclusion. Stream the music.

I started a NetRadio and broadcasted the Stereo Mix from my laptop to the MPD. Voila.

This way you can play Spotify on a device that lacks native Spotify support, but can play Internet Radio.
